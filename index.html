NewJeans' song 'Bubble Gum' is a vibrant and playful track that encapsulates the feelings of youthful love and excitement. The lyrics use the metaphor of bubble gum to describe the sweet, soft, and delightful emotions associated with a new relationship. The repeated references to the gum's characteristics—bouncy, sweet, and smooth—parallel the light-hearted and joyful experiences of young love.
